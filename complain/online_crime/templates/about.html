Online Crime Reporting Portal

About the Portal

The Online Crime Reporting Portal is a platform dedicated to making the reporting of crimes more accessible and efficient. It aims to empower individuals to report crimes quickly and effectively while providing them with real-time updates on their complaints. Our goal is to make the process of reporting crimes as smooth and transparent as possible, bridging the gap between citizens and law enforcement.

our goal is provide service, track the progress of their cases, and stay informed on the developments, all while ensuring data privacy and security. We are committed to leveraging technology to make communities safer and foster a collaborative relationship with law enforcement.

Key Features:

  • Instant submission of crime reports with descriptions, evidence, and location.
  • Real-time tracking of complaints with regular updates on progress.
  • User-friendly interface designed to provide seamless experience.
  • Secure, confidential, and private reporting system to ensure safety and protection of victims.

Our Vision:

Our vision is to create a secure and empowered community where every individual can report and track crime reports with complete trust in the process. We aim to reduce the barriers in crime reporting and ensure that no case goes unnoticed.
